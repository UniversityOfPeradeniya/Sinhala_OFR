Board of Directors

The Board of Directors holds ultimate responsibility for the Company’s value creation activities and is its apex decision making body. The Board comprises of 10 directors, of whom 05 function in an executive capacity and out of the remaining 05 non-executive directors, 05 are independent directors.

Management Team

Arpico Finance Company PLC. is spearheaded by its honorary chairman Mr. Bri Ponnambalam followed by a senior director board. The hierarchy drills down from Managing Director to a panel of Executive Directors and Independent Directors to the Chief Operating Officer to ensure that the organizational decisions are made to principally ensure the highest level of benefits to its clients.
